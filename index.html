The trip to Shanghai Normal University

ahaha,I have already several days not write blog, because I am so busy
this week, I have to take part in a competition in this Friday(It’s today)
, I have prepared two to three days in order that I can perform well.
Fortunately, This afternoon, when I stand on the rostrum, I am a little
nervous, but I did many times presentation about this project,
so quickly I read all words loudly just like I remembered all of contents.
after 4 minuts, I finished my presentation, The teacher began to ask me
question about this project, The first teacher asked me how many students
test this App, I answered him without any thought, 20-30 students
(I said casually, haha), then, the second teacher asked me what do you
think about your project if you put it into business model. I think it
for a moment, I said “Consider of students’s special reason, it’s too hard
for us to put it run on the Internet and let more people know us App,
because we have not a lot of money, But in this stage, our school are
encouraging students to do pioneering work, so we will perfect our App and
try to apply school innovation project, then we will put our App run on the
Internet”, I saw her noded her head. The third teacher asked me what is the
difference between Taobao and yours. This question was thought by me before
this competition. I told him The most difference is our object is unique–
farmer, and our function is also strong targeted. I saw he could not say more
than one word! hahahaha.
Finally, I answered all of their questions and I thought I do my best to this
project, So I want to say a word to d0zingcat “You owe my a meal”. haaaaaa
